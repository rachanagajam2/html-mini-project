Kashi Vishwanath:
The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva, one of the principal deities in Hinduism. The main idol in the temple is called Vishwanath, meaning "Lord of the Universe".It is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas, which are the most sacred shrines of Lord Shiva.Many Hindus believe that visiting this temple and taking a dip in the nearby River Ganga can wash away sins and help attain salvation (moksha).

Meenakshi Temple
The Meenakshi Amman Temple, located in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Shiva. Known for its stunning architecture, the temple features tall gopurams (towers) covered in colorful sculptures and a hall with 1,000 beautifully carved pillars. It is one of the most important temples in South India and a major tourist attraction. The temple celebrates the divine marriage of Meenakshi and Shiva during the Meenakshi Thirukalyanam festival, drawing thousands of devotees every year.
